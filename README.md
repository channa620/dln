# DLN Sarees - Traditional Elegance Webstore

Welcome to **DLN Sarees**, an elegant and responsive website designed to showcase and promote a curated collection of traditional Indian sarees. The platform combines aesthetic design, intuitive navigation, and a user-friendly contact system to connect saree lovers with timeless heritage fashion.

## ✨ Features

- **Responsive Home Page**  
  A welcoming section with brand introduction and a call to action.

- **Sarees Collection**  
  Beautiful display of Kanjivaram and chiffon party wear sarees with prices and "Order Now" buttons.

- **About Us Section**  
  A detailed overview of DLN Sarees’ origin, mission, and craftsmanship.

- **Contact Us Page**  
  Fully functional form with fields for name, email, phone number, and address — optionally linkable to WhatsApp or a database.

- **Modern Navigation**  
  Icon-enhanced navbar for intuitive access to all sections.
